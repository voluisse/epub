Can you see them there, by the side of the road,

not moving, not wrestling,

making a circle out of the space between the circles? Can you see them

pressed into the gravel, pressed into the dirt,

pressing against each other in an effort to make the minutes stop, to slow them down,

headlights shining in all directions, night spilling over them like gasoline in all directions, and the dark blue

over everything, and them holding their breath -

There is no way to make this story interesting.
