After work you go to the grocery store to get some milk and a carton of cigarettes. Where did you get those bruises? You don't remember.

Work was boring. You find a jar of bruise cream and a can of stewed tomatoes. Maybe a salad? Spinach, walnuts, blue cheese, apples, and

you can't decide between the Extra Large or Jumbo black olives. Which is bigger anyway? Extra Large has a blue label, Jumbo has a purple label. Both cans cost $1.29. While you're deciding, the afternoon light is streaming through the windows behind the bank of checkout coun-ters. Take the light inside you like a blessing, like a knee in the chest, holding onto it and not letting it go. Now let it go.
